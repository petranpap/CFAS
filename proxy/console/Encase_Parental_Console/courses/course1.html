Learning Objectives (LO)

  • LO1: Students can define and explain what cybersafety is
  • LO2: Be able to list the most common online risks and understand the harm they can cause
  • LO3: Understand the importance of being safe, responsible, and respectful online

Main Messages (M)

  • M1: Cybersafety is important for the online user's mental and physical health
  • M2: Anyone, even an expert online user, can experience online risks
  • M3: Acting safe and responsible online is as important as acting safe and responsible in real life

Example Questions (Q)

  • Q1: What is Cybersafety?
  • Q2: List the online risks they know
  • Q3: Do they feel comfortable and safe with your online activities?

Practical Example (P)

  • P1: Give real statistics (time spent, ages of users, reported risks, etc.) for online activities and risks.
